How it works

Lock Commit NARA. Earn NARA + ETH every 15 minutes.

Lock for up to one year. Weight is quadratic — locking twice as long earns dramatically more than twice as much. Earlier lockers face less competition and earn through the full warmup ramp.

Bond Deposit ETH. Receive more NARA than market price.

Buy discounted NARA in exchange for a vesting wait. ETH from bond purchases partially flows back as ETH rewards for lockers — bond buyers and lockers are aligned.

Arena Burn NARA. Compete for ETH prizes.

Pay ETH to enter. Burn NARA to move and push opponents. Compete for position, claimable ETH, and permanent on-chain reputation. All burned NARA is gone permanently — no recycling.

Why it's different

The reserve is sealed, not promised. 700,000 NARA sit in a contract where the withdrawal path is blocked by code, not by team policy. No multisig can override it.
Yield comes from protocol use, not inflation. Lockers earn ETH when bonds are purchased and when the arena generates fees. The mechanism is revenue-driven.
Commitment compounds. The quadratic weight function means locking 12 months doesn't earn 4× more than 3 months — it earns dramatically more. Time has real value here.
Four positions, one token. Hold for price exposure. Lock for reward flow. Bond for discounted size. Play for competitive upside and on-chain reputation.
