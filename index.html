How the core works

Finite reserve in motion

700,000 NARA is sealed in the reward reserve and admitted into the engine over time. The emission side is finite by design.

ETH is the expanding side

The same core accepts ETH from current and future protocol products, so new revenue surfaces do not need a new distribution system.

Commitment decides share

Locks become active weight. The more committed the position, the more of each epoch's NARA and ETH flow it earns.
